KANZAKI FONT FAMILY

Kanzaki is a display typeface sculpted with special curves inspired by old typography combined with semi-reversed contrast. A semi-serif font that has a classic and elegant feel, yet warm and friendly.

To beautify your creations, Kanzaki is equipped with various opentype features such as disrectionary ligatures, alternates, swashes and stylistic sets. Available in 5 weights: Thin, Light, Regular, Medium, Bold and its matching italics.

Perfect for branding, packaging, logotypes, labels and posters.
